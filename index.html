About Us

BornoPath is a free and open-source learning tool designed to help users strengthen their English vocabulary. Whether you're a student, language learner, or curious explorer, BornoPath provides a simple and interactive way to learn word meanings, synonyms, antonyms, and pronunciation—all in one place.

Our goal is to make vocabulary learning easier, faster, and more engaging for everyone. BornoPath is built with care using modern web technologies, and it’s continuously improved based on user feedback.

We’re committed to keeping BornoPath accessible, ad-free, and community-driven. Your words matter—follow the path.
Learn a little every day. Let your words grow — with BornoPath.
