About Us

BornoPath is an experimental project created by Towfik Ahmed to make English vocabulary learning simpler, smarter, and rooted in the learner’s language and culture. The name combines two Bangla words — “Borno” (letter) and “Path” (lesson) — reflecting our mission: to guide learners on a clear and confident path through the world of words. We believe that learning vocabulary doesn’t have to be overwhelming. BornoPath uses a friendly, focused approach to help you learn new words through daily practice, interactive tools, and progress tracking — all in a clean, distraction-free environment. This is just the beginning. BornoPath is a work in progress — shaped by curiosity, driven by passion, and open to improvement with every step.
Learn a little every day. Let your words grow — with BornoPath.
