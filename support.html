How Bornopath Works

Bornopath combines modern technology with effective learning methodologies to help you master English vocabulary efficiently.

Smart Dictionary

Access 19,000+ carefully curated English words with Bangla translations, synonyms, antonyms, and detailed definitions.

Adaptive Learning

Intelligent algorithms track your progress and focus on words you struggle with, using spaced repetition for optimal retention.

Progress Analytics

Comprehensive tracking of your learning journey with detailed statistics, accuracy metrics, and performance insights.

PWA Technology

Install as a native app on any device. Works seamlessly online and offline with automatic cloud synchronization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bornopath completely free to use?
Yes, Bornopath is completely free and will always remain free. It's an open-source project dedicated to making language learning accessible to everyone.
How to sign up to Bornopath?
To sign up, visit home page, Enter you Email address and simply click on the "Next" button. Follow the instructions to complete the registration process. (Email verification is required)
Didn't receive any verification email?
Please check your spam or junk folder. If you still can't find it, try resending the verification email from the sign-up page.
if the issue persists, contact support at towfikahmed2477@gmail.com.
How to install Bornopath Web app?
To install Bornopath as a web app, visit the installation guide here. Follow the instructions for your specific device and browser.
Can I use Bornopath on multiple devices?
Absolutely! Your progress is tied to your Firebase account, allowing you to seamlessly switch between devices while maintaining your learning data.
