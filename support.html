Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bornopath completely free?

✅ Yes, Bornopath is completely free with no hidden costs. We believe in making language learning accessible to everyone.

How do the different quiz modes work?

✅ We offer three practice modes: Quick (5-minute daily challenge), Customized (tailor to your level), and Unlimited (practice without restrictions). Each mode is designed to suit different learning needs and time availability.

Is Bornopath mobile-friendly?

✅ Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

How can I track my progress?

✅ Your profile includes a comprehensive dashboard that shows your learning progress, achievements, and leaderboard position. You can also see detailed statistics about your performance over time.
